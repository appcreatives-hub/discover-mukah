Festival

Tapak Kaul Mukah

A vibrant coastal site home to the annual Melanau Kaul Festival. It showcases unique cultural rituals like Tibau (Giant Swing) and Serahang, traditional performances, and local cuisine.

[Image: Jerunei Pole]
History

Jerunei Sungai Misan

A historical burial site featuring a jerunei pole carved from belian wood. These poles housed the remains of noble leaders and sacred offerings. Although the pole no longer stands, the site remains spiritually significant.

Culture

Sapan Puloh & Traditional Attire

The Mini Museum Sapan Puloh preserves artifacts like Terendak hats and fishing tools. Learn about the elegant traditional attire of Melanau women, featuring black velvet, gold ornaments, and songket sarongs.
